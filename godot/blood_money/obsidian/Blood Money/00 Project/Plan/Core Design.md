##### Core Loop
- On map, choose a room to go to
- Complete that room
- Move up a floor
- Repeat until you beat the final boss or until you die

##### Key Resources
- Health
- Energy
- Cards
- Time (if enemies get stronger over time, then that affects decision making)

##### Win / Lose Condition
- Win
	- If you beat the final boss
- Lose
	- If your health goes to 0 or below

##### Unique Selling Point
- Health and Gold are merged into the same currency, so more thought to shop purchases
- New cards, relics, enemies, events
